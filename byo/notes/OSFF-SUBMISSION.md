## Session Description (1200 characters)

Financial institutions have aggressive AI value targets, yet FINOS AI Governance Framework provides expert-curated structure to meet them responsibly. With the right operational infrastructure, Trustworthy AI becomes a powerful accelerator—ensuring consistent, scalable AI deployments with reduced friction. Today's opportunity lies in building AI governance operations to harness industry guidance at the speed and scale required.

This session presents a DevSecOps-ready reference implementation of AI governance and live demo. The "Linked Data Model Operate" (LinkMO) platform—built with LinkML-compatible patterns from bioscience communities—demonstrates operational governance, increasing financial services and cross-sector collaboration, advancing AI governance (baked-in bioscience CSR).

See AI governance operations through four access patterns: CLI for automation, REST API for integration, Web UI for exploration, and Graph DB for relationship analysis. Watch automated risk discovery, mitigation mapping, regulatory cross-referencing, and evidence generation. Extensible patterns you can adapt—"Start Left": consume open governance standards early.

Business impact: scale AI without scaling governance costs, reduce friction between delivery and control teams, pursue AI targets with compliance confidence. Complete open-source reference architecture—working code you can deploy, adapt, and operate today.
